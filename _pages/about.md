Currently, I am a PhD student at the University of Tübingen, Germany. I am part of the <a href="https://www.mlsustainableenergy.com/">Climate, Energy and Machine Learning Systems</a> and the <a href="https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/informatik/lehrstuehle/methoden-des-maschinellen-lernens/start/">Methods of Machine Learning</a> research groups. My research is at the intersection of Machine Learning and Climate Science — in particular, I apply generative machine learning methods such as diffusion models to perform statistical downscaling. I also did a research visit at <a href="https://mila.quebec/">Mila</a> in Montreal, Canada.

Feel free to reach out in English or German if you are interested in climate modeling or related topics.
