ML emulators have the potential to overcome the high computational costs of climate models, yet their effective use remains challenging. In our new <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22320">arXiv preprint</a> (ongoing work), we reflect on how to bridge this gap. If you are working in this area, we would be very happy to connect and exchange ideas. We will also be at EGU in Vienna and Climate Informatics in Lausanne.
